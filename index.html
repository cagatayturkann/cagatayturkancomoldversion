About

I'm a junior front-end/web developer who graduated from computer engineering. In college, I've tried and developed projects various of technologies such as mobile, network etc. but I changed my path to front-end and web development field after the Linux Summer Camp. I was a participant of a front-end programming class.

In the direction of the internships that I did and the courses that I attended, I have improved my English in a technical way and regular way with written and verbal. Especially I think that; appproaching humans with empathy, being positive and good-humored, giving value of team work, working peacefully and harmoniously with working area will help my in my working life and I aim to rise by developing myself in a long term.
